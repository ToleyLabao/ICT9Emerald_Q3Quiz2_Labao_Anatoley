Synopsis
Focuses on the early years of King Henry VIII's nearly 40-year reign of England. This show looks at Henry's female companions like Katherine Howard and Anne Boleyn. It delves into his relationships with people such as Sir Thomas More, Cardinal Wolsey, and Charles Brandon, Duke of Suffolk, who was Henry's best friend and unofficial adviser.
Set in the late 16th century, the series revolves around the life of Mary, Queen of Scots, and her rise to power in the French court. The series stars Adelaide Kane as Queen Mary Stuart, alongside an ensemble cast. Mary rallies, readying herself to rule and trying to find a balance between the demands of her country and those of her heart.
Young Catherine the Great enters a disastrous marriage to Emperor Peter III of Russia, and being a royal woman living in rural Russia during the 18th century is hard. Marrying an emperor means being forced to choose between her own personal happiness and the future of Russia. If the crown fits... take it.
